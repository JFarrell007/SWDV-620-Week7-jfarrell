The President is Missing

BY Bill Clinton and James Patterson

This thrilling novel will keep you up at night. Washington is in a state of fear as enemies plan to take down the US. Espionage and Cyberterror wreak havoc on the Nation and an inside person is furthering the cause. A traitor is in the midst. You thought your prepper neighbor was crazy. After reading this book you will be digging your own underground bunker preparing for the worst of times. Could this could really happen? Former President Bill Clinton and bestselling author James Patterson make fictious events seem authentic. Can the leader of the Free World save the Western civilization?
